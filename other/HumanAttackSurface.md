# Human Attack Surface

## Overview
Humans are often the weakest link in security. This topic explores
how social engineering and human behavior can be leveraged or
manipulated in offensive security.

## Topics
- Trust exploitation: authority, urgency, curiosity triggers
- Common social engineering techniques
- Case studies (ethical and theoretical)
- Human error combined with system weaknesses
- Preventive mindset: simulating and understanding human mistakes

## Takeaway
Understanding human behavior is critical to map and anticipate
potential attack vectors without targeting real individuals.
